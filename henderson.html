Where is Henderson?

Nevada's second-largest city — located immediately southeast of Las Vegas, nestled between the Spring Mountains and Lake Mead National Recreation Area.

Nevada's Premier Suburb

Where Suburban Luxury
Meets Desert Living

Henderson combines the tranquility of suburban living with access to luxury amenities in a perfect blend. From gated communities and golf course estates to family-oriented neighborhoods with scenic views, Henderson offers homes that suit a variety of tastes and lifestyles, perfect for anyone planning to come here.

As Nevada's second-largest city and one of the fastest-growing communities in the nation, Henderson has been recognized repeatedly as one of America's safest and most livable cities. Master-planned neighborhoods like Green Valley Ranch, Anthem, and Lake Las Vegas set the standard for desert luxury living.

Whether you're drawn by the award-winning schools, resort-style amenities, or the proximity to all Las Vegas has to offer — while enjoying a quieter pace of life — the Crighton Rinaldi Team brings deep local expertise to every corner of Henderson's diverse real estate market.

What Makes It Special

Living in
Henderson

🏡

Suburban Living Excellence

Henderson combines the tranquility of suburban living with access to luxury amenities. From gated communities and golf course estates to family-oriented neighborhoods with scenic mountain and city views — there's a home for every lifestyle.

🎓

Schools & Education

Henderson is home to a variety of highly regarded public and private schools. Neighborhoods are thoughtfully designed with schools located conveniently nearby, giving parents peace of mind and children a strong foundation for academic success.

🏥

World-Class Healthcare

Residents have access to modern healthcare facilities within Henderson and the surrounding area. Leading hospitals like Henderson Hospital and St. Rose Dominican Hospitals provide comprehensive services from routine checkups to specialized treatments.

🌊

Parks & Recreation

Lake Las Vegas provides scenic waterfront activities, boating, and dining. Railroad Pass Park and Cornerstone Park offer sports fields, walking trails, and picnic areas. Golf lovers enjoy world-class courses like Revere Golf Club and Rio Secco Golf Club.

🛍️

Shopping, Dining & Entertainment

The District at Green Valley Ranch is a premier open-air shopping and dining destination. Galleria at Sunset offers a wide selection of stores and entertainment. Residents also enjoy proximity to Las Vegas' world-class nightlife and entertainment.

✈️

Accessibility & Transportation

Harry Reid International Airport is nearby for domestic and international travel. Major highways, including Interstate 515 and US Routes 93 and 95, make commuting to Las Vegas and beyond convenient. Well-maintained roads make daily travel effortless.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions
About Henderson

The questions buyers ask most when exploring Henderson real estate.

What is the median home price in Henderson?
+
The median home price in Henderson is approximately $450,000 as of 2026, though prices vary widely by neighborhood and property type. Entry-level single-family homes in communities like Cadence and Inspirada typically start in the $350K–$500K range. Master-planned communities like Green Valley Ranch and Seven Hills typically range from $500K–$1.5M+, while luxury estates in Lake Las Vegas and MacDonald Ranch can exceed $3M+.
Is Henderson a safe city to live in?
+
Henderson consistently ranks as one of the safest large cities in America. It has been named to multiple "safest cities" lists over the years by publications like Money Magazine and WalletHub. The city's master-planned communities, active neighborhood watch programs, and dedicated police force contribute to exceptionally low crime rates compared to national averages for cities of its size.
What ZIP codes cover Henderson?
+
Henderson is covered by several ZIP codes: 89002 (Basic area), 89011 (Lake Las Vegas area), 89012 (Green Valley Ranch, Seven Hills), 89014 (Green Valley), 89015 (central Henderson), 89044 (Anthem, southern Henderson), and 89052 (Anthem, MacDonald Ranch). When searching for homes, search across multiple ZIP codes to see the full inventory available in the city.
Are there 55+ communities in Henderson?
+
Yes — Henderson has several highly regarded active adult and 55+ communities. Sun City Anthem is one of the most popular, offering resort-style amenities, golf, and an active social calendar. Four Seasons at Inspirada and Tuscany Village also offer age-restricted living with HOA-managed maintenance and community centers — ideal for retirees and snowbirds seeking a lock-and-leave lifestyle.
What are the best neighborhoods in Henderson for families?
+
Henderson's top family-friendly neighborhoods include Green Valley Ranch (established master-planned with top schools), Anthem (guard-gated with resort amenities), Inspirada (vibrant new community with parks and events), and Cadence (modern master-planned with excellent connectivity). Each offers proximity to highly-rated schools, community parks, and family amenities. The Crighton Rinaldi Team can help you match the right neighborhood to your family's specific priorities.
What is the property tax rate in Henderson?
+
Nevada has some of the lowest property taxes in the nation. Henderson properties fall under Clark County's property tax rate, which is approximately 0.7%–0.9% of assessed value annually — significantly lower than most U.S. states. Nevada also has no state income tax, making the total tax burden on homeowners extremely competitive. Exact rates vary based on the property's taxing district. Your Crighton Rinaldi Team agent can provide current tax estimates for any property you're considering.
How hot does it get in Henderson, and is it livable year-round?
+
Summer temperatures in Henderson regularly reach 105°F–110°F in July and August. That said, Henderson's higher elevation (up to 2,200 ft) makes it marginally cooler than the Las Vegas Strip. The dry desert heat is far more tolerable than humid climates, and most homes feature pools, quality HVAC, and energy-efficient construction. The ideal seasons — October through April — offer warm days, cool nights, and stunning desert landscapes. Henderson is absolutely livable year-round for those who embrace the Southwestern lifestyle.
Is Henderson a good place for real estate investment?
+
Henderson has demonstrated strong long-term appreciation driven by population growth, quality of life rankings, no state income tax, and steady demand from both local and out-of-state buyers. The rental market is solid, particularly near UNLV's graduate campus and major employment corridors. New construction communities like Cadence and Inspirada continue to attract significant buyer interest. Note that investment performance varies significantly by property type and location — the Crighton Rinaldi Team can help you identify investment-grade properties and analyze potential returns.
